Frequently asked Question

We will let the community know as soon as we can but we are anticipating a launch sometime towards the end of February.
We are making Shintsha available on WhatsApp and SMS for the initial release, and as soon as we are confident with our USSD version, we will then make this publicly available.
We are currently engaged in conversations with leading custody services like BitGo, and Luno as soon as we reach an agreement, we will make this publicly known. We have also been conducting our own internal research for the past two years on a non-custodial solution, but this is still very infant.
Users will be able to purchase cryptocurrencies through Mobile Money, as this is our primary focus for the early stages of the platform, but we intend to add the ability to purchase using a bank account in the future. The cryptocurrencies which will be made available at first are Ethereum (ETH), Bitcoin (BTC), Ripple (XRP) and Ethereum Classic (ETC), as the project matures we intend on adding more and potentially access to Defi protocols.
For all users, we require that they provide us with their first name, last name, as well as a selfie of them holding a picture of their identity document front and back facing. However, this will be a bit different for users without smartphones; we are still exploring other solutions for this use case.
